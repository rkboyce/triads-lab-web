# Content Model

## Pages

## Home

Purpose: Give a credible first read of the lab and route users quickly.

Sections:

- Hero with lab name, one-sentence mission, primary links.
- Research theme grid.
- Featured project or tool.
- Recent publications/news.
- Training callout.
- Contact band.

## Research

Purpose: Explain the lab's intellectual scope.

Sections:

- Medication safety for older adults.
- Clinical decision support.
- Pharmacovigilance and signal detection.
- Natural product-drug interactions.
- Knowledge bases, ontologies, and generative models.
- Evidence generation and implementation.

## Projects

Purpose: Show active and past work.

Fields:

- Title
- Status
- Funding/source if public
- Collaborators
- Methods
- Outputs
- Related publications
- Contact person

## Publications

Purpose: Let visitors scan, filter, and cite work.

Fields:

- Title
- Authors
- Venue
- Year
- DOI/PMID/arXiv URL
- Tags
- Project links

Recommended filters:

- Clinical decision support
- Pharmacovigilance
- Natural products
- Knowledge graphs
- Older adults
- Training

## People

Purpose: Present PI, staff, students, and alumni.

Fields:

- Name
- Role
- Affiliation
- Research interests
- Links: email, ORCID, Google Scholar, GitHub, LinkedIn

## Training

Purpose: Highlight student and trainee routes into the lab.

Sections:

- AIBIDS undergraduate research experience
- Current student opportunities
- Mentoring areas
- Expected background
- How to contact

## Contact

Purpose: Provide simple routes to the lab and DBMI.

Fields:

- PI contact
- Department address
- Lab or admin email
- DBMI link
- GitHub organization/project links when available
